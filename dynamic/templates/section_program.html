Our Program

The Leadership Training Institute is for students who want to change the lives of others through their leadership. We challenge everyone to affect their world and grow as leaders. Throughout the program our students create, plan, and implement projects in their community. From creating bags to helping newly adopted children adapt to their new homes to ...; students are given the resources and knowledge to affect change.

As an organization, LTI believes in the value of pay it forward. Our students receive one-on-one mentoring while developing leadership skills. They then design and implement their own project that targets an issue they observe in their community.

{{ html.image_tag('flowchart.png') }}

The program is free to all high school students, and each student accepted into the program receives $150 to work on their project. If the financial burden is too great for a student to travel to MIT, LTI will fully subsidize the cost of travel. Students must attend all twelve sessions to be a part of the program, though two absences are allowed.
